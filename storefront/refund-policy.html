Refund Policy

We stand by the quality of our artisans' work. If you are not satisfied, we are here to help.

Our 14-Day Guarantee

If your piece arrives damaged or does not meet your expectations, we offer a full return within 14 days of delivery. The item must be in its original, unused condition.

Process for Returns

To initiate a return, please contact our concierge at support@artisanat.gallery with your order number. Once approved, we will provide you with a fully insured return shipping label.

Exemptions

Custom commissions and personalized limited editions are exempt from our standard return policy.
